About IEEE RVU Student Branch

IEEE RVU Student Branch is a dynamic community of engineering students dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. Established with the vision to create a platform for students to enhance their technical knowledge and professional skills, IEEE RVU SB has been at the forefront of organizing impactful technical events.

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation of innovators and leaders in technology. Through workshops, seminars, competitions, and hackathons like Vikasya, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Hackathon Rounds

Vikasya comprises two challenging rounds designed to test your creativity, technical skills, and problem-solving abilities.

Round 1: Ideation Challenge

The first round focuses on idea generation and conceptualization. Teams will submit their project proposals addressing one of the provided problem statements.

  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Submission Requirements: Project abstract, feasibility analysis, and basic prototype design
  • Judging Criteria: Innovation, feasibility, impact, and alignment with the problem statement

In this round, teams will:

  • Choose one problem statement from the provided list
  • Develop a comprehensive project proposal
  • Create wireframes or mockups of the proposed solution
  • Prepare a 3-minute pitch video explaining their concept
  • Submit all required documents through the hackathon portal

Top 20 teams will advance to Round 2 based on the evaluation by our expert panel of judges.

Round 2: Build & Deploy

The second round is where ideas transform into reality. Selected teams will build a working prototype of their solution and present it to the judges.

  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Submission Requirements: Working prototype, technical documentation, and presentation
  • Judging Criteria: Functionality, technical complexity, user experience, and presentation

In this round, teams will:

  • Develop a functional prototype of their proposed solution
  • Create comprehensive technical documentation
  • Prepare a business model and scaling plan
  • Deliver a 5-minute final presentation followed by Q&A
  • Demonstrate the working prototype to judges and attendees

Winners will be selected based on the final evaluation, with emphasis on innovation, execution, and potential impact.

Rules & Regulations

To ensure fair play and maximize the learning experience, all participants must adhere to the following rules.

General Rules

  • Teams must consist of 2-4 members.
  • All team members must be enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program.
  • Cross-college teams are allowed and encouraged.
  • All code and designs must be created during the hackathon duration.
  • Use of open-source libraries and APIs is permitted with proper attribution.
  • Plagiarism or use of pre-existing projects will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Round-Specific Rules

Round 1 Rules

  • Teams must choose one problem statement from the provided list.
  • Abstract must not exceed 500 words.
  • Ideation submissions must be made before the round 1 deadline.
  • Each team is allowed to submit only one proposal.
  • Teams must indicate any external dependencies or resources required for their solution.

Round 2 Rules

  • Only selected teams from Round 1 will participate in Round 2.
  • Teams must continue with the same project idea approved in Round 1.
  • All team members must be present during the final presentation.
  • Teams must submit their code to a GitHub repository and make it accessible to judges.
  • Any use of AI tools must be disclosed and explained in the documentation.

Problem Statements

Explore the exciting challenges that await you at Vikasya. Choose one and bring your most innovative solutions.

Smart Urban Transportation System

Domain: IoT & Smart Cities

Design and develop an intelligent transportation system that addresses urban mobility challenges. Your solution should integrate real-time data analytics, IoT devices, and user-friendly interfaces to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and enhance the overall commuting experience.

Key Considerations:

  • Real-time traffic monitoring and prediction
  • Integration with existing transportation infrastructure
  • Optimization algorithms for route planning
  • User-friendly mobile application for commuters
  • Sustainability and environmental impact

AI-Powered Healthcare Assistant

Domain: Healthcare & AI

Develop an intelligent healthcare assistant that enhances patient care through personalized monitoring, early diagnosis support, and treatment adherence. Your solution should leverage artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics to provide meaningful insights to both patients and healthcare providers.

Key Considerations:

  • Health data collection and analysis
  • Personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Early warning systems for potential health issues
  • Medication and treatment adherence tracking
  • Privacy and security of sensitive health information

Sustainable Agriculture Technology

Domain: AgriTech & Sustainability

Create a technology-driven solution to enhance agricultural productivity while promoting sustainable farming practices. Your solution should address challenges such as resource optimization, crop monitoring, climate resilience, and supply chain efficiency.

Key Considerations:

  • Precision farming techniques and technologies
  • Resource optimization (water, fertilizer, energy)
  • Climate-smart agricultural practices
  • Data-driven decision support for farmers
  • Supply chain transparency and efficiency

Cybersecurity Defense System

Domain: Cybersecurity & Network Protection

Design and implement an advanced cybersecurity defense system that can detect, analyze, and mitigate emerging threats in real-time. Your solution should leverage cutting-edge technologies to protect digital infrastructure from sophisticated cyber attacks.

Key Considerations:

  • Real-time threat detection and analysis
  • Automated response and mitigation strategies
  • Machine learning for anomaly detection
  • User behavior analytics
  • Comprehensive security dashboard and reporting
