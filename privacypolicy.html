Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6th, 2025

1. Introduction

IEEE RVU Student Branch ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you participate in our hackathon event, Vikasya 2025.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the website or participate in the event.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about participants in our hackathon, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, contact number, educational institution, course of study, year of study, and profile picture.
  • Team Information: Team name, member details, and project information.
  • Technical Data: Internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, operating system, and platform.
  • User Content: Project submissions, presentations, code repositories, and any other material submitted as part of the hackathon.
  • Images and Videos: Photographs and videos taken during the event for documentation and promotional purposes.

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect information directly from you when you:

  • Register for the hackathon
  • Submit project details
  • Communicate with organizers
  • Participate in surveys or feedback forms
  • Use our website
  • Attend the physical event

4. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing your registration and facilitating your participation
  • Communicating with you about the hackathon
  • Evaluating and judging submissions
  • Analyzing participation and improving future events
  • Creating promotional materials about the event
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Sharing Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to:

  • Event Sponsors and Partners: Information about participants and their projects may be shared with our sponsors and partners for evaluation, prize distribution, and potential recruitment opportunities.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as registration platforms, hosting services, and communication tools.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with other third parties with your consent.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at ieee-rvu@rvu.edu.in.

8. Children's Privacy

Vikasya 2025 is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information.

9. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: ieee-rvu@rvu.edu.in
Address: IEEE RVU Student Branch, RV University, Bengaluru - 560059, Karnataka, India
