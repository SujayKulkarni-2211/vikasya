Code of Conduct

Last Updated: April 6th, 2025

1. Respect and Inclusion

Vikasya is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone. Discrimination or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

2. Expected Behavior

Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate. Communicate openly, but thoughtfully. Value diverse perspectives and experiences, and engage in constructive dialogue.

3. Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, bullying, hate speech, discrimination, or any form of intimidation is strictly prohibited. This includes offensive language, inappropriate imagery, or disruption of sessions and activities.

4. Reporting and Enforcement

Violations of this code can be reported to any organizer or volunteer. Reports will be handled confidentially. The organizing team reserves the right to remove individuals for misconduct without refund or recourse.

5. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all participants, including attendees, organizers, mentors, sponsors, and volunteers at all Vikasya-related events, both in-person and online.
