Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vikasya?
Vikasya is a premier hackathon organized by IEEE RVU Student Branch, designed to challenge participants to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. The name "Vikasya" symbolizes growth and development, representing our commitment to fostering technical excellence and creativity.
When and where will Vikasya 2025 be held?
Vikasya 2025 will be held from April 12 to April 14, 2025. Round 1 will be online, while Round 2 will be conducted at RV University, Bengaluru.
Who can participate in Vikasya?
Vikasya is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any recognized educational institution. Teams must consist of 2-4 members, and cross-college teams are allowed and encouraged.

What are the prizes for Vikasya 2025?
Vikasya 2025 offers prizes worth over $10,000, including:
  • 1st Place: $5,000
  • 2nd Place: $3,000
  • 3rd Place: $2,000
Additionally, all participants will receive participation certificates, and finalists will get special recognition.
What technologies can we use?
You are free to use any technology, programming language, or framework of your choice. The only requirement is that your solution should address the chosen problem statement effectively.
Will accommodation be provided?
Yes, for teams selected for Round 2, accommodation will be provided at RV University campus for the duration of the hackathon. Details will be shared with the selected teams.
Can we use pre-existing projects?
No, all projects must be developed from scratch during the hackathon. You can come with ideas and plans, but all coding and design work must be done during the hackathon period.
